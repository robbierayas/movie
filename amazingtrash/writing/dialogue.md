## PROJECT OVERVIEW

A dark thriller about a homeless chemist (Adam) forced to dispose of bodies for a billionaire serial killer (Geoff). When Adam tries to fight back, the system protects the wealthy and crushes the poor.

## WORLD-BUILDING NOTES

### Setting
- Near-future dystopia
- Extreme wealth inequality
- Contactless payment (phone tapping) instead of cash
- Tracktown: homeless encampment in railyard
- Amazing Company: Geoff's corporation with shipping/logistics

### Technology
- Auto-cars (self-driving vehicles)
- Drones for surveillance and attacks
- Robot enforcers (Moosk)
- Holograms
- Contactless payment systems

### Social Structure
- Wealthy elite untouchable by law
- Homeless communities marginalized
- Police corruption
- Night patrols at Tracktown perimeter
