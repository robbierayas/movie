# Scene 11 - Parallel Preparation (MONTAGE)

## TYPE
Parallel Montage / Intercut

## PURPOSE
Show both Liam and the Reeves family preparing for the final confrontation. Establish the contrast between Liam's healing/planning and the family's transformation.

## TIME SPAN
Days to weeks

## MONTAGE STRUCTURE

### Shot 1 - Liam
Liam in a hiding spot, treating his wounds from the fight with Hank.

### Shot 2 - Reeves
Sarah and Eli building traps, using Hank's prepper supplies.

### Shot 3 - Liam
Liam studying maps, notes—learning from his mistakes, planning his return.

### Shot 4 - Reeves
Emma and Olivia helping reluctantly, exchanging worried looks.

### Shot 5 - Liam
Liam testing himself physically—healing, regaining strength.

### Shot 6 - Reeves
The house transformed: hidden weapons, trip wires, defensive positions.

### Shot 7 - Both
Split screen or intercut: Liam approaches. The family waits.
