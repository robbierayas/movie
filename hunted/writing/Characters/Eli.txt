## CHARACTER ARC
- **Beginning State (Act 1)**: Serious kid, takes training seriously. Trying to be cool while still being young. Pride in his skills.
- **Inciting Incident**: Witnesses father's murder, sister held hostage. Frozen with horror but anger building.
- **Escalation (Act 2)**: Goes all-in on mother's revenge mission. Helps track Liam, fights him at apartment, prepares traps. "Dad's dead." No hesitation.
- **Crisis Point (Act 3)**: Traps Liam inside—"TWO MEN ENTER! ONE MAN LEAVES!" But can't wait for him to come to them.
- **End State**: Dies confronting Liam. "I'm not Dad" proves tragically true—he wasn't ready. His death is the cost of shared obsession.

## DIALOGUE STYLE
- Trying to sound mature/cool in Act 1 ("I've been practicing", sibling banter with Emma)

- Movie references when escalated ("TWO MEN ENTER! ONE MAN LEAVES!")
- Last words are for his mother: "Mom... run..."
