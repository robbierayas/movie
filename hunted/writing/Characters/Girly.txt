# GIRLY

## BASIC INFORMATION
- **Full Name**: Gorilla "Girly" Reeves
- **Age**: ~4-5 years (adult dog)
- **Breed**: Rottweiler
- **Family**: The Reeves family pet

## ARCHETYPE
**The Protector** - Loyal family guardian who defends to the death.

## CHARACTER FUNCTION
Represents the family's unity and normalcy. Her presence in Act 1 shows domestic warmth. Her tasing in Act 3 signals the beginning of Liam's assault. Her death alongside Bobby represents the loss of all outside help—the family is truly alone.

## PHYSICAL DESCRIPTION
- Rottweiler—black and tan markings
- Muscular, ~80 pounds
- Intimidating appearance but sweet with family
- Well-trained, responds to commands

## PERSONALITY TRAITS
- Loyal and protective
- Gentle with the children
- Loves to play (especially with Bobby)
- Good hunting companion—has rhythm with Hank and Bobby from hunts
- Will override commands to protect family

## RELATIONSHIPS
- Hank: Master, trained her, hunting partner
- Sarah: Loved
- Children: Protective, playful
- Bobby: Hunting buddy, roughhouses with her
- Liam: The threat she dies fighting

## CHARACTER ARC
- **Act 1**: Happy family pet. Plays during cookout. Represents the family's peaceful life.
- **Act 3 (Scene 13)**: Runs out to protect family from Liam. Gets tased. Left for dead.
- **Act 3 (Scene 15)**: Wakes up when Bobby arrives. Fights alongside him. Ignores command to stay when Bobby is killed. Dies attacking Liam. Loyal to the end.

## KEY SCENES
- **Act 1, Scene 4: Family Time** - Bounds around during cookout. Bobby calls her "the best gorilla"—Olivia corrects him. Plays during self-defense training. Begs for scraps at dinner. Beloved family pet.
- **Act 1, Scene 5: The Surveillance** - Observed by Liam through binoculars. He notes the Rottweiler.
- **Act 3, Scene 13: The Return** - Charges Liam as he approaches the house. Gets tased. Left motionless on the ground. "Good dog."
- **Act 3, Scene 15: Bobby's Return** - Wakes up when Bobby checks on her. They hunt together one last time—the rhythm from dozens of hunts. Commanded to stay but ignores it when Bobby catches fire. Attacks Liam viciously. Killed with a pipe. Dies protecting her family. Loyal to the end.
